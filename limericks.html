The Story of Mia

There once was a bunny with feet
The bunny had feet made of meat
She put on her belt
Twas crafted with felt
Then chopped up the buffalo beet.

The bunny then met up with snoc
The snoc had a weapon of rock
She bonged bunny's head
Then filled it with lead
And used all the fur to make sock.

The snoc used her sock made of fur
She gifts it to kot, who then purrs
"This sock is so great!"
The kot then just ate
The snoc who was sad as a burr.
